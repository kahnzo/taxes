# 2026 Estimate of Federal Income Tax

A simplified calculator estimating your United States Federal Income Tax liability. The calculator is tailored toward individuals nearing retirement with no children. Income sources are pensions, social security, 401(k) distributions, and W-2 income.

In addition to estimating your net income, the page will include helpful guidelines for filling out W4, W4P, W4R, and W4V forms.

| Tax Form | Description | Purpose | Reported On |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| W4 | Employee's Withholding Certificate | Provide to Employeer | W4 |
| W4V | Voluntary Withholding Request | Provide to Social Securtity Administration | SSA-1099 |
